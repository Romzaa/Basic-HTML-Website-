Introduction

Throughout my career in the automotive field, I have taken on a wide range of projects that combined technical expertise, diagnostics, and advanced ECU calibration. Each project reflects my ability to analyze complex systems, implement tailored solutions, and deliver measurable improvements in performance and reliability.

Below are selected highlights of my work:


Key Projects:

1. BMW M54B25 - Custom Stage 1 ECU Calibration

  • Designed and implemented a Stage 1 performance tune on Siemens MS43 ECU.
  • Optimized ignition and injection maps to achieve a stable AFR target of 13:1.
  • Delivered measurable improvements in drivability, efficiency, and responsiveness.



2. BMW E92 - N55 Engine Swap & ECU Reprogramming

  • Executed a complete N55 engine swap using an E90 donor system.
  • Performed full ECU flash and EEPROM adaptation, ensuring VIN compatibility and immobilizer alignment.
  • Achieved seamless integration with factory systems and restored OEM reliability.



3. Bosch MED9.1 - Exhaust Acoustics Enhancement

  • Engineered a custom calibration to enable selective pops & bangs feature.
  • Developed safe activation strategies via factory controls (AC button, cruise control, rear defrost).
  • Enhanced driver engagement while maintaining daily usability and system integrity.



4. Bosch ME9 - Advanced Diagnostics Development

  • Identified, defined, and structured Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) tables within WinOLS.
  • Improved the accuracy of ECU fault tracing and calibration mapping.
  • Contributed to faster diagnostics and more efficient tuning processes.



5. BMW MS41 - Flash Upgrade & System Research

  • Conducted in-depth research on MS41 ECU hardware/software versions and flash memory constraints.
  • Analyzed compatibility between MS41.0 and MS41.1 firmware structures.
  • Documented a safe flashing procedure, supporting future upgrade and development work.



Brief

These projects represent my dedication to continuous learning, innovation, and precision in automotive engineering. By combining technical expertise with a structured and analytical approach, I have consistently delivered results that improve performance, reliability, and efficiency. I look forward to applying this same mindset and skillset to future challenges.
